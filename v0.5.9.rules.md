# v0.5.9 Rules — Fromax No.55 Visuell Bockguide

---

## Isolering — guiden får inte påverka befintlig funktionalitet

RULE: v0.5.9 is visual guide only.
RULE: Do not change kaplängd.
RULE: Do not change marks.
RULE: Do not change bend deduction.
RULE: Do not change calculate() logic — only append fgSyncFromCalc() call at the end.
RULE: Do not add new geometry constants to the calculation engine.
RULE: Do not add alternative math paths to the calculation engine.
RULE: Do not add new warning types or severity levels.
RULE: Do not affect S-böj, Flerböj, Svanbock, Takoffset, or Takrör calculations.
RULE: Existing bananböj calculate() result values are read-only in the guide.
RULE: applyWarningHierarchy() is not touched by v0.5.9.

---

## Rendering

RULE: Render with Canvas — no image files.
RULE: No external dependencies — all JS and CSS must be inline.
RULE: The guide has exactly 3 steps: Placering, Böj, Slutresultat.
RULE: Bananböj is one bend — do not add rotation or second-bend steps.

---

## Matematikgräns — rörgeometri vs. ritmatematik

### Rörgeometri (befintliga helpers — inga inline-reimplementationer)

RULE: Pipe offset/height values displayed in the guide must come from geParallelOffset() — not recalculated inline.
RULE: Bend deduction values displayed in the guide must come from geBendDeduction() — not recalculated inline.
RULE: The guide must not reimplement pipe geometry formulas.

### Ritmatematik (tillåtet i guiden)

RULE: Math.sin, Math.cos, Math.atan2 and similar are allowed inside the guide's drawing and animation code.
RULE: Arc coordinates, pixel positions, animation frame values — these are rendering concerns and are unrestricted.
RULE: The distinction is: if the value is shown to the user as a pipe measurement, it must come from a shared helper. If the value is only used to position a pixel on screen, it may use any math needed.

---

## Fromax-orientering (kritisk korrekthet)

RULE: Fromax 0-mark is the absolute reference — always rendered in gold (#e8a020), never toned down.
RULE: Degree arc/circle (gradskiva) belongs under the pipe, not above.
RULE: Klon (grip jaw) must be shown in step 1, positioned against the pipe on the inward side of the bend.
RULE: Three reference marks on the tool handle must be shown — first mark aligns with 0°.
RULE: Mirrored tool orientation (Höger vs Vänster) must produce geometrically mirrored canvas output.
RULE: Long piece of pipe goes in the side-selector direction — left for Vänster, right for Höger.

---

## Fjädring / Springback

RULE: fgSyncFromCalc() must read the #springback field value.
RULE: FG.calc.bendAngle = nominal angle + springback — this is what the user bends to.
RULE: Step 2 animation must animate to bendAngle, not to nominal angle.
RULE: A green dashed line and label must show where the pipe lands after release (nominal angle) when springback > 0.
RULE: If springback = 0, no springback annotation is shown — no empty labels.

---

## Synk med beräkning

RULE: fgSyncFromCalc() is called at the end of calculate() — no other injection point.
RULE: fgSyncFromCalc() reads: r-angle, r-m1, kapa-value, springback, banana-clamp-lead-result.
RULE: All read operations are non-destructive — no DOM writes outside the guide's own elements.
RULE: If calculate() has not been called, the guide renders with selected angle from buttons (FG.angle) and no calc values.

---

## Animationsregler

RULE: Step 2 must animate using ctx.arc — not two straight lines meeting at a point.
RULE: Step 2 animation must show a proper rounded bend arc.
RULE: Step 3 must also use ctx.arc for the bend section — no straight-line approximation at the bend.
RULE: Animation uses ease-in-out cubic — no linear or jump transitions.

---

## Stegregler

RULE: Step 1 must show a pipe drop zone (highlighted channel) with "Lägg röret här" label before rendering the pipe.
RULE: Step 1 must show klon, tool arm with 3 marks, gradskiva, pipe with long side labeled, M1 mark at 0.
RULE: Step 1 M1 mark must have a red glow (ctx.shadowBlur) to make it visually dominant.
RULE: Step 1 must show the 0-mark with a gold glow ring and "REFERENS" label — this is the most important element.
RULE: Step 1 must NOT show CLR annotation, even in Advanced mode — CLR belongs in steps 2 and 3.
RULE: Step 1 must show ghost wrong-placement mark (semi-transparent) with ✕ fel label.
RULE: Step 1 must show "bocka först" red banner when banana-clamp-lead-result is visible.
RULE: Step 1 description must include: "Markeringen visar var böjen startar — inte mitten av röret."
RULE: Step 1 description must include: "Ingen rotation behövs för denna böj."
RULE: Step 3 must show H-stycke fitting at left (0 mm reference) and T-koppling fitting at exit.
RULE: Step 3 result row must show actual values when calc is done — not hardcoded illustrative values.

---

## Interaktivitet

RULE: Angle selector allows only 30°, 34°, 45° — no freetext input.
RULE: Side selector allows only Vänster and Höger.
RULE: Mode switch must not reset selected angle or side.
RULE: Step navigation must not reset selected angle or side.

---

## Mode-regler

RULE: Simple Mode must not show CLR annotation.
RULE: Advanced Mode must show CLR annotation with arrow in steps 1 and 2, and bend deduction in step 3.

---

## Sessionshantering

RULE: Guide is collapsed by default — controlled by sessionStorage key fromax_guide_open.
RULE: fromax_guide_open = '1' means open; missing or empty means collapsed.
